Our Mission

Our school d.a.v.inter collage, kundol, agra is a co-educational institute providing quality education to the deprived section of society. It is running from class 1 to xii recognized and aided by u.p. government from 1960.we are also providing co-carricular activities to the children like sports and games, scoute and guide and n.c.c jd and sd wings. There are 800 children in this session 2020-21.Our mission is to produce educated, disciplined and honest citizens for new India.

We work hard to prepare every student for their professional life

The Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education (RTE) Act, 2009 provides for access to elementary schools for children of the age group of 6-14 years, within the defined area or limits of neighbourhood. Section 6 of the Act provides that ‘the appropriate government and local authorities’ shall establish, within the area or limits of a neighbourhood, a school, where it is not already established, within a period of three years from the commencement of the Act. In pursuance of Section 6 of the Act, the Central RTE Rules has notified the area or limits of neighbourhood as one kilometre, within which a primary school and 3 kilometre within which an upper primary school, has to be established. The States have notified area or limits of their neighbourhood norms in their state RTE Rules, factoring in their State specific condition, for opening of schools. As reported by States/UTs in their Annual Work Plan, 2018-19, 97.15% of habitations in the country are covered by primary schools and 96.49% of habitations are covered by upper primary schools
